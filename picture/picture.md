# Picture

## White background

Create an image in landscape aspect, rendered in a cinematic photorealistic style, featuring the interior of a space station that is extremely bright, white, and immaculately clean. The architecture is sleek and futuristic, with smooth white surfaces, soft ambient lighting, and minimalist design. Reflections and highlights emphasize the sterile clarity of the space. The perspective should feel immersive and expansive, with symmetrical corridors, glowing panels, and subtle metallic accents. The overall mood is calm, futuristic, and pristine, evoking a sense of advanced technology and quiet isolation. Cinematic sci-fi realism with high-key lighting, clean geometry, and minimal color. Emphasis on brightness, spatial clarity, and futuristic elegance.​

## Glowy City

Create a high-resolution night-time cityscape photograph capturing reflections in wet streets. Wide-angle lens for perspective distortion, neon lights creating vibrant color contrast. Shallow depth of field isolates reflective puddles, bokeh lights blur background buildings. Symmetrical geometric composition with street lines and building facades forming strong visual rhythm. Tyndall effect visible through mist or humidity, soft diffused highlights. Storytelling via human silhouettes and illuminated environment, cinematic framing. Aspect: square.​

## Video

Ferramenta recomendada: Acesse o [Luma Dream Machine](https://lumalabs.ai/dream-machine) ou o [Kling AI](https://klingai.com).
Upload: Use a imagem que você me enviou como "Starting Frame" (Imagem inicial).
Cole o seguinte prompt (em inglês, que essas IAs entendem melhor):

```txt
The man in the blue jumpsuit starts in the exact pose as the image. He slowly and confidently crosses his arms over his chest. After crossing his arms, his expression transitions into a subtle, intelligent, and satisfied smile. He gives a very slight, slow nod of approval. The cinematic lighting and hangar background remains consistent. High quality, 4k, realistic movement.
```

O que vai acontecer no vídeo:

- Início: Ele parado como na foto.
- Ação 1: O movimento fluido de cruzar os braços.
- Ação 2: A mudança de microexpressão facial (o olhar "inteligente" e o sorriso satisfeito).
- Ação 3: O leve aceno de cabeça no final.

Dica extra: Se usar o Luma ou Kling, certifique-se de marcar a opção "Maintain Character" ou "End Frame" (se você tivesse uma foto dele sorrindo) para manter a fidelidade do rosto. Como você só tem uma foto, o prompt acima é o suficiente para a IA deduzir os movimentos.
